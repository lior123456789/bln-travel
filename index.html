The Process

Eighty-six steps. Six weeks.

One case. Built by the same four pairs of hands, start to finish.

Origin

Made in Berlin.

Our atelier sits in a former typewriter workshop in Kreuzberg. Every BLN piece begins with raw aluminum sheet and ends with a hand-signature on the inner lining.

Read Our Story →
01
Design

Hand-drawn. Then proved.

Every silhouette starts on paper. Mock-ups are 3D-printed, weight-balanced, and dropped from six feet a hundred times before a millimeter is committed to metal.

02
Engineer

Cut from a single sheet.

Each panel is CNC-milled from a single billet of 7000-series aluminum. The wave pattern is press-formed, not stamped — preserving the alloy's structural integrity.

03
Craft

Leather. Stitched by hand.

Full-grain vegetable-tanned leather from a fourth-generation Tuscan tannery. Each corner is cut, skived, stitched, and burnished by hand — four hours per case.

04
Inspect

Eighty-four checks.

Wheel rotation. Latch tension. Hinge alignment. Seam pressure. Every case passes 84 individual checks before it earns the BLN signature on the inner panel.
